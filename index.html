Undervolt — Sapphire Nitro+ RX 9070 XT

AMD Adrenalin · V/F Curve · Lower temps, quieter fans, better sustained clocks

0 of 12 steps complete
Setup
1
Open AMD Adrenalin → Performance tab → Tuning
2
Change Tuning Control from Auto → Manual
3
Enable GPU Tuning toggle
4
Click Advanced to open the V/F curve editor Curve plots Frequency (X axis) vs Voltage (Y axis)
Apply the Undervolt
5
Find the rightmost point on the curve — this is max frequency/voltage
6
Drag it down to reduce voltage — safe starting point: drop ~100mV from stock Target: ~2900 MHz @ 1000mV as a first attempt
7
Click Apply
Test Stability
8
Run Furmark or Unigine Superposition for 15–20 minutes
9
Stable, no crashes? → Drop another 25–50mV and retest
10
Crashed or artefacts? → Increase voltage by 25mV and retest
11
Repeat until you find the lowest stable voltage Most 9070 XTs land between 900–1000mV depending on silicon lottery
12
Play a demanding game for 30+ mins as final real-world stability check ⚠ Stress tests pass ≠ always game stable. Real-world test is mandatory.
